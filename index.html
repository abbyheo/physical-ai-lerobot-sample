What is included

Each episode follows the LeRobot folder structure:

  • data: frame-level parquet with joint states, commanded actions, and timestamps
  • meta: info.json, stats.json, tasks.parquet, and episode-level parquet under episodes/
  • videos: three MP4 streams (observation.images.wrist, .top, .belly)

episode_context.json contains the collection context for each episode: session ID, task and scenario IDs, robot platform, collection method, episode duration, status, and segment-level review outcomes.

Two episodes, not a benchmark. The point is to make the data-collection part of the case study checkable.
